# Examples

## Example workflow

1. load a scenario
2. initialize actor states and constraints
3. run decision rounds
4. evaluate payoffs and state transitions
5. persist traces, summaries, and comparison metrics

## Example use cases

- sanctions escalation and industrial-policy response
- regulation shock and sector reallocation
- cross-border strategic rivalry and capital repricing
- institutional reform under prolonged external pressure

## Example scenario

**Input:** tighter EU regulation combined with rising US-China strategic tension  
**Question:** how do states, firms, and market participants reallocate under different assumptions about coordination, risk tolerance, and policy credibility?
