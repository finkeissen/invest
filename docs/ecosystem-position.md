# Ecosystem Position

This repository is best treated as a **standalone project**.

It may share methodological themes with broader research work around explicit assumptions, modular reasoning, and traceable hypothesis formation, but it should not depend on external repositories in order to be understandable or usable.

## Practical stance

- no submodule dependency
- no required companion repository
- no hidden conceptual prerequisite for contributors

## Why keep this note

The point of this document is only to clarify positioning:

- **Invest-GT should stand on its own**
- related research traditions may inform its style
- implementation, documentation, and validation should remain self-contained here
